Vex Robotics Club

  • Description: Gain a strong founcdation in robotics engineering as we explore design concepts to more advanced robotics systems using the VEX EDR Robotics kit or the VEX IQ Robotics kit
  • Categories: Robotics, Engineering Design, Coding, Mathematics, Science
  • Grades: 4-8

3D Printing

  • Description: In this club, students will learn how to create their own three-dimensional designs that they can animate and print using a 3D printer! By the end of this series, they’ll be able to take home a 3D-printed version of their design!
  • Categories: Engineering Design, Science, Mathematics, Critical Thinking, Creative Thinking
  • Grades: TK-8

Coding Club: Python

  • Description: Explore the basic principles of computer science, while learning how to code in Python, a widely used and easy-to-learn programming language.
  • Categories: Coding, Computer Science, Logic, Mathematics
  • Grades: 4-8

Game Design Club

  • Description: Work with other students to design and create a multi-level online game using a variety of gaming platforms.
  • Categories:Game Design, Coding, Logic, Science, Mathematics, Critical Thinking, Creative Thinking
  • Grades: TK-8

LEGO Engineering Club

  • Description: Investigate different architectural designs and experiment with simple and compound machines using a variety of LEGO kits including the LEGO We-Do 2.0 kit, the LEGO Simple & Powered Machines kit, and the LEGO Mindstorms EV3 Robotics kit. Emphasis will not only be on the Engineering Design Process, but on the Mathematics and Science that are observed in each project.
  • Categories: Engineering, Mathematics, Science, Critical Thinking, Creative Thinking
  • Grades: TK-8

K'NEX Engineering Club

  • Description: Design and create a variety of engineering projects from bridges and catapults to automated rollercoasters.
  • Categories: Engineering Design, Science, Mathematics, Critical Thinking, Creative Thinking
  • Grades: TK-8

Filmmaking Club

  • Description: Do you love movies? Ever wonder how they’re made? Join the Filmmaking Club to experience the magic of making your own movie! Learn camera and lighting techniques, acting and script writing, story boarding, video editing, and green screen technology.
  • Categories: Filmmaking, Engineering, Critical Thinking, Creative Thinking
  • Grades: TK-8

3D Animation Club

  • Description: Have ideas for a cartoon or comic book? Come and make your own work of art through CGI! Use 3D software to model and animate your own world!
  • Categories: Geometry, Computers, Engineering Design, Mathematics, Critical Thinking, Creative Thinking
  • Grades: 2-8
