Vex Robotics

  • Categories: Robotics, Engineering Design, Coding, Mathematics, Science
  • Grades: 4-8

3D Printing

  • Description: In this club, students will learn how to create their own three-dimensional designs that they can animate and print using a 3D printer! By the end of this series, they’ll be able to take home a 3D-printed version of their design!
  • Categories: Engineering Design, Science, Mathematics, Critical Thinking, Creative Thinking
  • Grades: TK-8

Coding: Python

LEGO Engineering

  • Description: Investigate different architectural designs and experiment with simple and compound machines using a variety of LEGO kits including the LEGO We-Do 2.0 kit, the LEGO Simple & Powered Machines kit, and the LEGO Mindstorms EV3 Robotics kit. Emphasis will not only be on the Engineering Design Process, but on the Mathematics and Science that are observed in each project.
  • Categories: Engineering, Mathematics, Science, Critical Thinking, Creative Thinking
  • Grades: TK-8
